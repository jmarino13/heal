Targeted peptide therapies

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that can support healing, recovery, performance, and healthy aging when used responsibly under medical supervision.

BPC-157

Healing • Inflammation • Gut repair

Considered for patients focused on recovery and gut support, BPC-157 may be incorporated into protocols aimed at soft-tissue repair and inflammation.

TB-500

Recovery • Flexibility • Tissue repair

A thymosin beta-4 derivative that may be used to support mobility, flexibility, and overall musculoskeletal recovery under physician guidance.

GHK-Cu

Anti-aging • Skin health • Hair support

Frequently used in topical and injectable forms, this copper peptide is known for supporting collagen, firmness, and scalp health.

Glow & Wolverine Blends

Synergistic repair & radiance

HEAL signature blends designed to support global tissue repair, resilience, and aesthetic outcomes when clinically appropriate.

NAD+

Energy • Focus • Cellular support

NAD+-focused protocols may be used to support mitochondrial health, mental clarity, and overall vitality, with close clinical monitoring.

GLP-1 weight-management programs

Our GLP-1 programs are reserved for patients who qualify based on medical history, labs, and body-composition goals. Dr. Raina and Jude pair medication with realistic lifestyle support to help you build sustainable change.

  • Comprehensive intake and lab review
  • Individualized GLP-1 dosing strategies
  • Ongoing check-ins and side-effect monitoring
  • Guidance on nutrition, movement, and habits

Is GLP-1 right for you?

GLP-1 medications are not appropriate for everyone. Our team screens for contraindications such as certain thyroid tumors, pancreatitis history, and medication interactions before prescribing.

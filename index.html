Targeted peptide therapies

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that can support healing, recovery, performance, and longevity when used responsibly under medical supervision.

BPC-157

Healing • Inflammation • Gut repair

Often used to support soft-tissue healing and gut integrity, BPC-157 may be considered for patients focused on recovery and chronic inflammation.

TB-500

Recovery • Flexibility • Tissue repair

A synthetic fragment of thymosin beta-4 that can be incorporated into protocols aimed at musculoskeletal recovery and mobility goals.

GHK-Cu

Anti-aging • Skin health • Hair support

Frequently used in topical and injectable forms, this copper peptide is known for supporting collagen, skin firmness, and scalp health.

Glow & Wolverine Blends

Synergistic repair & radiance

Proprietary blends such as Glow and Wolverine combine targeted peptides to support global tissue repair, recovery, and aesthetic results.

NAD+

Energy • Focus • Cellular support

NAD+–focused protocols may be used to support mitochondrial function, mental clarity, and healthy aging, always under clinical review.

Peptides are prescribed only after a complete clinical evaluation. Individual results may vary.

Is GLP-1 right for you?

GLP-1 medications are not appropriate for everyone. Our providers carefully screen for contraindications such as certain thyroid tumors, pancreatitis history, and other medical conditions before prescribing.
